Our Leadership

Fone Dynamics is headed by a trio of innovators representing the best and brightest in telecommunications technology.

Shane Mitchell

CEO/Founder

Shane started his career as a Self-Employed Developer. He tackled a range of complex business and systems design problems and created systems using a variety of programming languages, operating systems and technologies.

In 2011, he was appointed the Technical Director of Fonecall, a telecommunications company where he was primarily responsible for building and maintaining mission critical systems.

Shane’s expertise lays in orgnaisational strategy, systems design, software architecture, database design, centralised and decentralised distributed networks, mathematics, electronics and automation. He was appointed CEO of Fone Dynamics in 2017.

Qualifications: BE, Mechatronics, First Class Honors (UQ)

Jordan Grives

Director/Founder

With over 10 years’ experience in providing bespoke telecommunication products and services, the former CEO and Managing Director of telecommunications company Fonebox Group. Jordan lead the group to obtain a prosperous and rewarding exit to Nasdaq-listed J2 Global in October 2016. Jordan is recognised as a seasoned entrepreneur and as such is finalist and winner of many accolades, including: National Finalist for 2016’s Ernst & Young ‘Entrepreneur of the Year’, Winner of the Brisbane Business News’ 2016 Entrepreneur of the Year and Finalist for Lord Mayor’s Young Business Person of the Year 2015 and 2016 to name a few.

Paul Briggs

General Manager

A tertiary educated executive with very refined skills and seasoned experience has served as a Director CEO, CIO, CTO, COO, GM and Corporate Governor across numerous technology based high-growth businesses including multiple domestic and international Telstra Business and Deloitte Fast 50 award winning companies.

Qualifications: B.BUS Double Major in both Business Management and IT Management (USQ), MBA (UQ), Post Graduate Diploma in Corporate Governance (GIA), MAICD, MGIA, MACS (snr cp).
